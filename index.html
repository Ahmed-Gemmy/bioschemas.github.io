Bioschemas

Bioschemas is an open initiative aiming to engage the life science community to provide structured data using schema.org. Bioschemas seeks to reuse existing schema.org types (and propose new types when necessary) relying on existing community agreements and standards formats. Bioschemas also aims to provide a layer of quality and compliance on top of schema.org via specifications recommending vocabularies, cardinality and minimum fields within types.
