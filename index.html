BioSchemas

BioSchemas is a joint initiative from several organizations and comunities in life science that relies and extends from schema.org in order to facilitate the description, information sharing and promotion of life science activities. BioSchemas is hoping to encourage life science organizations to adopt schema.org markup, since it doesn't require programming skills, it is widely adopted and well documented, and it makes sense anyway for SEO (Search Engine Optimization). It is then possible to scrape web pages in order to access consistently formatted information.

Visit the About Us page if you want to know more about BioSchemas.

You can also follow BioSchemas on Twitter or fork the BioSchemas repository on GitHub.

Collaboration

BioSchemas is an open community and welcomes any organization or individual to join this community effort. Members of BioSchemas groups have regular calls as well as face-to-face meetings, so if you want to get in touch, join a group or provide feedback please have a look at the Meetings page or feel free to contact directly a group of interest through the Joining BioSchemas page. For general information about BioSchemas, send an email to all@bioschemas.org.
