Standard Group

BioSharing, a curated, searchable web portal of inter-related data standards, databases, and policies in the life, environmental and biomedical sciences, is working to support schema.org markup in its pages to faciliate discovery by search engines.

This group investigates the required extensions to schema.org to support such use case.
