Commons Group

The Bioschemas Data Repositories Group provides a way to describe common properties across all the Bioschemas specifications.

Products

Specification

The Life Science Data Repositories specification is the main product of this group

Living document Google doc 0.1

Membership

The Commons Group is open to any organization or individual willing to contribute to the goals of this group. To become a member please follow the instructions to "Join a group".

Members and contributors

Members and contributors are listed in the Bioschemas contributors spreadhseet. If you think your name is missing please feel free to add a comment with your contact details in a new row of the spreadsheet.

Communication

Use the commons@bioschemas.org mailing list for internal and external communication. More information about groups and communication channels in the Groups page.
