Tools Group

The Tools Group develops and maintains a community specification for describing life science tools.

This group investigates the required extensions to schema.org to support such use case.

Membership

The Tools Group is open to any organization or individual willing to contribute to the goals of this group. To become a member please follow the instructions to "Join a group".

Members and contributors

Members and contributors are listed in the Bioschemas contributors spreadsheet. If you think your name is missing please feel free to add a comment with your contact details in a new row of the spreadsheet.

Communication

Use the tool@bioschemas.org mailing list for internal and external communication. More information about groups and communication channels in the Groups page.
