Training Material Group

The Bioschemas Training Material Group develops and mantains a community specification for describing training materials in biosciences available on the internet.

Training materials tend to be dispersed in an unstructured way around the Internet. They are mainly available off manually crafted web pages with a general lack of programmatic interfaces. Even though some training material providers make efforts to curate and structure their content, materials are still not described in a consistent manner that would make them easy to discover, exchange or compare programmatically.

Purpose

  • To develop a common schema to facilitate the description and dissemination of life science training material, using schema.org.
  • To define a set of metadata and vocabularies, built on top of existing technologies and standards, that can be used to represent training materials in web pages and applications.
  • To make it easier to discover, exchange and integrate life science training material information across the internet.

Specifications

The current specifications can be found in the Training Material Specification document, or you can check the entire list of Bioschemas Training Material properties. Usage examples can be found on the Examples page.

Communication

Use the training-material@bioschemas.org mailing list for internal and external communication. You can apply to join this mailing list or see previous messages on the Google group Training Material web page.

Tasks and issues related to this group are available in GitHub issues section tagged with "type: training material".

This group has regular calls as well as face to face meetings with the rest of Bioschemas groups. These can be followed on the Meetings page.
