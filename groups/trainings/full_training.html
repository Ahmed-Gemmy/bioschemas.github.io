Training Material Properties

Here you can find all BioSchemas Training Material new properties, as well as existent schema.org Training Material properties and properties inherited from the Thing specification.

Proposed new properties for the Training Material type

Property Expected Type Description Cardinality Content Guideline Controlled Vocabulary
level Text Difficulty level; level of experience of users in the subject. One Recommended Yes
pid Text Permanent identifier, such as DOIs. One Optional
